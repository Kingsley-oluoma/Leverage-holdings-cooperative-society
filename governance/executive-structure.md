# Interim Executive Structure

## Chairman

Responsible for leadership, strategic direction, governance oversight, and representation of the Cooperative.

## Vice Chairman

Supports the Chairman and acts in their absence.

## Secretary

Responsible for administration, records, correspondence, and meeting documentation.

## Treasurer

Responsible for financial administration, reporting, and stewardship of Cooperative funds.

## Board Members

Provide oversight, strategic input, and support the achievement of Cooperative objectives.
