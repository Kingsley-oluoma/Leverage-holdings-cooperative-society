# Founding Cohort

Leverage Holdings Multipurpose Cooperative Society was established in 2026 by an initial group of twelve founding members.

The founding cohort collectively committed to building a sustainable, member-owned institution focused on wealth creation, responsible investment, transparency, and long-term value generation.

The founding members represent a diverse range of professional backgrounds including healthcare, engineering, business analysis, entrepreneurship, and other sectors.

This document serves as a historical record of the Cooperative's formation.
